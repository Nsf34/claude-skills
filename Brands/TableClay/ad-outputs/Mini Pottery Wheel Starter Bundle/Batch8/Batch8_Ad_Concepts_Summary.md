## Product Spec Sheet

**Product Name:** Table Clay Mini Pottery Wheel Starter Bundle

**Physical Description:** A compact tabletop electric pottery wheel with a smooth 4–6" spinning wheel head and a removable splash tray. Clean cream/off-white body with soft, rounded modern design — not a toy, but not intimidating. The bundle includes 2 lbs of air-dry off-white clay, 16 professional shaping and carving tools, a paint set with brushes, and an instruction booklet. Fits comfortably on any table.

**Colors:** Wheel body in warm cream (#F5F0E8); clay in natural off-white/beige; brand accent terracotta (#C4826A); sage green (#B5C4A1); deep charcoal (#2D3436).

**Must NOT look like:** A children's brightly-colored plastic toy, a large industrial pottery wheel, a kitchen appliance. Must NOT show kiln equipment, uncontrolled messy clay splatter, or handles on the wheel body.

**Key Proof Points:**
- Everything included — wheel, clay, 16 tools, paints
- No kiln, no class needed
- Results in under 20 minutes
- Beginner-friendly, seriously satisfying
