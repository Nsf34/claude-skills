### Concept Details

#### C1 — "Meet Your New Obsession" (24/25)
- **File:** Batch3_C1_Meet_Your_New_Obsession.jpg
- **Angle:** Lifestyle / Aspirational
- **Visual:** Moody, dramatic close-up of the mini pottery wheel with a freshly thrown clay bowl. Dark atmospheric background, warm directional lighting, cinematic shallow depth of field.
- **Headline:** MEET YOUR NEW OBSESSION
- **Font Style:** Modern condensed sans-serif, bold white uppercase
- **Color Palette:** Terracotta, cream, warm brown
- **Why It Works:** Taps into obsession/passion psychology. The dramatic product photography elevates perceived value and creates an aspirational feel that drives impulse purchases.

#### C2 — "Made By Me" (24/25)
- **File:** Batch3_C2_Made_By_Me.jpg
- **Angle:** UGC-Style
- **Visual:** Overhead flat-lay of small handmade pottery pieces arranged on a rustic wooden surface beside the mini wheel. Natural daylight, casual styling with clay dust and tools visible.
- **Headline:** MADE BY ME
- **Font Style:** Hand-lettered style, warm white
- **Color Palette:** Natural wood, terracotta, soft white
- **Why It Works:** UGC-style authenticity drives trust. The pride-of-creation angle resonates with the maker movement and positions the product as an enabler of personal expression.

#### C3 — "Unbox Your Studio" (24/25)
- **File:** Batch3_C3_Unbox_Your_Studio.jpg
- **Angle:** Unboxing
- **Visual:** Styled unboxing shot with the mini pottery wheel emerging from premium packaging. All accessories and clay supplies artfully arranged. Clean white background with soft shadows.
- **Headline:** UNBOX YOUR STUDIO
- **Font Style:** Clean modern sans-serif, dark charcoal
- **Color Palette:** White, kraft brown, terracotta accents
- **Why It Works:** Unboxing content is among the highest-performing ad formats on social. Shows the complete bundle value proposition and triggers the excitement of receiving a gift.

#### C4 — "Your Kitchen Table. Your Studio." (22/25)
- **File:** Batch3_C4_Your_Kitchen_Table_Your_Studio.jpg
- **Angle:** Lifestyle
- **Visual:** Warm lifestyle scene of the mini pottery wheel set up on a kitchen table. Morning light streams through a window. Coffee mug nearby, casual home environment.
- **Headline:** YOUR KITCHEN TABLE. YOUR STUDIO.
- **Font Style:** Elegant serif, warm white
- **Color Palette:** Warm wood, cream, soft golden light
- **Why It Works:** Overcomes the "I don't have space" objection by showing the product fits seamlessly into everyday home spaces. Relatable setting lowers the barrier to purchase.

#### C5 — "The Hobby That Loves You Back" (22/25)
- **File:** Batch3_C5_The_Hobby_That_Loves_You_Back.jpg
- **Angle:** Emotional / Lifestyle
- **Visual:** Warm, cozy living room scene with the mini pottery wheel on a coffee table. Knitted blanket, freshly made clay mug, golden hour lighting. No people visible but personal touches suggest someone just stepped away.
- **Headline:** THE HOBBY THAT LOVES YOU BACK
- **Font Style:** Clean serif, warm white
- **Color Palette:** Cream, sage green, warm earth tones
- **Why It Works:** Emotional hook positions pottery as self-care and stress relief. The warm, inviting scene creates an emotional connection that drives engagement and shares.

---

### Strategic Notes
- **Angle Diversity:** This batch deliberately spans multiple proven DTC ad angles (aspirational, UGC, unboxing, lifestyle, emotional) to enable effective A/B testing across audience segments.
- **Visual Consistency:** All images maintain the Table Clay earth-tone brand palette while varying mood and setting to test different emotional triggers.
- **Platform Optimization:** All images generated in 1:1 square format optimized for Instagram feed and Facebook ad placements.
